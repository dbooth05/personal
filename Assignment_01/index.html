Dog Catalog

Welcome to the dog catalog!

The Austrailian Shepherd or "Aussie" was ironically a breed from the United States, that is medium-sized, come in many colors, intelligent and high energy working dogs great for active families.

The Corgi is a small and agile dog breed known for its size, playful nature, and their affection and compasion. They will always bring a smile to the whole family.

A Golden Labrador is one of the most common dog breeds, known for their size, color, and ability to bring smiles to everyones face. They are also common emotional support dogs, and used in search-and-rescue.

The Siberian Husky is what most people think when asked what breed sled dogs are. They are loyal, medium-sized, and friendly. On a side note, they are also known for their vocal nature.
