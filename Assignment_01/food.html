Food Catalog

Welcome to the food catalog!

This is a cheeseburger with a side of fries. You can have it as just a patty with cheese or you can add extra toppings such as lettuce, pickles, tomatoes, or onions. Some common condiments that can be used to add more flavor are ketchup and mustard. The typical side to this main dish are french fries to make it a delicious meal.

Donuts are a pastry enjooyed for their sweet, indulgent flavor. Typically circular in shape with a center hole, they are made from a rich dough that is fried until golden brown, resulting in a crsipy exterior and soft, fluffy interior. Donuts come in a variety of flavors and styles, ranging from classic glazed to powdered sugar.

Pancakes are a breakfast staple enjoyed for their simplicity and versatility. Made from a batter typically consisting of flour, eggs, milk, and baking powder, pancakes are cooked on a griddle or frying pan until golden brown and fluffy. maple syrup, butter, fruit, and chocolate chips are traditional toppings that add a lot to the sweet taste.

Pasta carbonara is a classic Italian dish known for its creamy texture and rich flavor. It typically consists of cooked pasta, often spaghetti or fettucine, tossed with a sauce made from eggs, cheese, and either pancetta or bacon. The dish is often seasoned with black pepper and somtimes garlic for added depth of flavor. Other vegetables are great possible toppings for this dish that can be eaten for either lunch or dinner.
